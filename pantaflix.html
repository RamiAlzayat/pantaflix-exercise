WHO WE ARE

Pantaflix was started by a group of experienced and successful filmmakers who believe the world deserves better when it comes to finding and watching film & TV. We're here to make it easy for you to watch what you know you love and discover things you'll never forget. Here's what you can expect from Pantaflix:

find what you love

From big-budget movies to cult classics, festival winning shorts to the latest documentaries, we've got them all, and lots more.

link abroad?

Expats of the world, get excited: Pantaflix is a great place to find films from back home you can't watch anywhere else.

rent for 48 hours

Once you rent something, you have 30 days to start watching it, then 48 hours after you first click play to watch it as many times as you want.
